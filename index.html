About me

My name is Ida Olsson and I am a 27 year old from Mariestad, Sweden. As a person I am outgoing and social, kind, a good friend and listener.
I grew up on the Isle of Thor with my parents and two siblings. I have a college degree in Health Science, I am a certified personal trainer
and health coach. I am a cat person, master sonderer and an oldest sibling in a nutshell. Forever too hard on myself
but always ready to go to battle for others.

Why I am studying NMD

I have always loved creating things. Painting, taking pictures, writing... Since I was very young I have been the one with fantasy and creativity to spare. But being raised in a family of doctors and nurses, this has always made me feel a bit different. I didn't pursue this and instead tried to fit in by choosing to study and work in the health field. I realized pretty quickly that even though I found it interesting, the subject didn't give me much else. I kept working on my small business where I was selling digital design on the side, creating it for fun, and the customers kept giving me amazing reviews and kept telling me that I have the eye for design. One day in March of 2022 I turned to my family and asked if they think I should apply for university again, but this time majoring in digital design. They all told me what I needed to hear. That it made all the sense in the world. I never thought they thought that, but with their support and a bit of courage, I applied and got in. I know now that I want to become a graphic designer. I want to work with companies and branding, copywriting and keep on expanding my own small business selling digital products. I believe NMD will help me in reaching these dreams.
